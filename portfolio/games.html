Unity

I ve always looked with astonishment at game design world but I never had tried to apply myself in it, luckily I had the occasion to try with a university project that regarded game design and photogrammetry. So I started practicing with the recreation of an existing island (brown's island) Then I keep practicing with terrain modelling and with the recreation of small buildings.

Main part of our project was the creation of a real-world object starting by taking photos, for reconstruc it later with the computer, (aka photogrammetry) more precisely bad photos (to make everything a little harder). We had to reconstruct blurred photos with matlab and then thanks to Zephyr by 3DF recreate the object from that photos. After some more operations we were ready to import the statue to our project.
